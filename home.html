Hello,world!
or why I decided to start a blog

We all have these days. We want to do something new, we feel inspired or we just don't have something better to do. The thing is, for me this the so-called creative time when I don't do something productive truly, I just create something I like.

Usually what I do is write down some small app that's a no-big-deal or go for a walk in the park. But today is kind of different - I decided that it's time to sharpen my html/css/bootstrap skills and what better way than building a site?

That's why this is my Hello World! to real html creation. I hope you enjoy it and maybe feel at least a bit inspired to make your next time-wasting project at least kind of meaningful.

P.S. Don't expect anything big from this blog - for now, it's just something I'm building for the purpose of creation. It probably won't be pretty nor complete but well, if you're here, just enjoy it, test it and maybe write to me with some feedback.
